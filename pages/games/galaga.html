Galaga
Arcade remake

Description

Who doesn't now Galaga?! Just for formality: Galaga is a space shooter game that was very popular in arcades. The player controls a space chip that must destroy all aliens that appear in it's view. Enemies will shot back, dive in your direction and try to abduct the player's chip. Every wave defeated will increase the difficulty.

This game has a lot of versions. This remake is focus on Galaga 1981 for arcades.

Production


Motivation

My specialization's professor has given me a lesson work. It was a tasks to remake an arcade game. In a list with Space Invaders, Pitfall and others, I choose Galaga. Other requirement was to make it using Monogame (open source version o XNA).

Galaga has some easy and hard issues. One of the easy was the fact that I have a few physics collisions and constraints. One of the hard one to me (and for everybody in the class that choose Galaga) was create and manage the formations. It needed a lot of math formulas!
