The Magic Tower
2D brawl game

Description

Wizards with the ability to conjure elements to cast powers will fight against waves of monsters inside the tower. Sometimes, they brawl with each other to decide who is the best caster.

Combining elements, like fire with water to use steam, the player can craft power to destroy its target. Adding fire to any power will cause burning in any creature hit, including the wizard whom cast it.

Production


Motivation

I've imagined who cool it would be if Magicka mixed with TowerFall Ascention. The creation process was different than any other I've already experienced. I have to mix two solid and stable games in one without know if it would be fun as well.

As the project was going further, I saw some future on it. The current state doesn't have a lot of magics and combinations, but already demonstrate a good gameplay.

Creating all the art and animation, like in Sprawl Celerity, make the project much harder. I'm growing up in this area but with a cost of patience and time.

Strong points

- Unet (Unity Network);

- Custom Shader;

- Tiled integration;

- All 3D objects created by me (except the goblin enemy, created by Matheus);

- Personal Framework;
